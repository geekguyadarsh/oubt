Minorities Enrichment Programs
Aiding the minorities in moving towards a viable and rewarding future
Our Minority Enrichment programs aim at enabling minorities to navigate various employability challenges. These programs equip minorities with necessary skills and tools by integrating them into an ecosystem that supports sustainable employability.

Empowering the minorities to progress and be future-ready

The estimated population of the United States of America is 328.2 million, and the minorities constitute to 39.4 percent of people, while the unemployment rate of them approximates to 13%.

Minorities Enrichment Programs
